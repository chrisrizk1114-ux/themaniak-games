Owner account — The Maniak
==========================

## Login credentials (default)

After running the owner seeder:

  Email:    themaniak.owner@gmail.com
  Password: ManiakOwner#2026

Change these in production via environment variables:

  OWNER_EMAIL=your@gmail.com
  OWNER_PASSWORD=your-secure-password
  OWNER_NAME=Site Owner

## Create / update owner account

  php artisan migrate
  php artisan db:seed --class=OwnerSeeder

On Render, the Docker start script runs OwnerSeeder automatically after migrations.

## Owner panel URL

  /owner

Only accounts with role "owner" can access this area. The navbar shows a "Owner" link when logged in as owner.

## Owner capabilities

- Dashboard with live stats (users, online, friendships)
- Manage all users (search, promote, demote, delete)
- View and remove any friendship
- Full player access (games, friends, online status)

## Security notes

- Regular sign-up always creates a "user" role, never owner
- You cannot delete your own account from the owner panel
- At least one owner must always remain on the site
- Change the default password immediately after first login
